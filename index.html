About me

Hi, and welcome! I am a Ph.D. candidate in economics at the California Institute of Technology, advised by Omer Tamuz. My research interests lies in the field of microeconomic theory, with a specific focus on information and social learning.

Before joining Caltech, I received my Bachelor of Economics (with First Class Honors) from the University of Queensland in 2018, advised by Priscilla Man and Jeffrey Kline. Prior to that, I studied Economics at Shandong University, China.
